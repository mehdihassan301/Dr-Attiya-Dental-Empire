Our story

Built on precision, run with warmth.

Dr. Attiya's Dental Empire began as a single chair on Siraj-ud-Daula Road with one promise: treat every patient like family, and treat every tooth like it matters. Today it is a full dental practice in Bahadurabad, Karachi — but the promise hasn't changed.

Our clinicians combine internationally trained technique with an unhurried, listen-first approach, so procedures that used to feel intimidating start to feel routine. From routine cleanings to full smile reconstructions, every plan is built around your comfort as much as your outcome.

What we treat

Complete dental care, one address.

Every service below is delivered chairside by our own clinicians — no outsourcing, no unfamiliar faces mid-treatment.

General Checkup & Cleaning

Routine exams, scaling and polishing to catch issues early and keep your smile healthy year-round.

Root Canal Therapy

Comfortable, single-visit endodontic treatment to relieve pain and save the natural tooth.

Orthodontics & Braces

Traditional braces and clear aligners for children and adults, planned around your timeline.

Emergency Care

Same-day appointments for dental trauma, sudden pain or broken restorations.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacement with titanium implants and precision crowns.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Whitening, veneers and bonding designed around your natural facial proportions.

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle, patient-first care that helps children build a lifetime of good dental habits.

Oral Surgery

Wisdom tooth extraction and minor surgical procedures under local anaesthesia.
